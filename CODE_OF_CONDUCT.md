# Code of Conduct

ChatWithAnyone should be a respectful space for contributors and users.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and direct.
- Assume good intent while still naming problems clearly.
- Respect privacy and personal boundaries.
- Keep feedback focused on the project and the work.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block users; or take other reasonable actions to protect the project community.

If you see a problem, contact the maintainer through GitHub. For security issues, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead of opening a public issue.
