# Support

ChatWithAnyone is an early-stage open-source project.

## Questions

Open a GitHub issue with the `question` label and include:

- Xcode version.
- iOS version and device or simulator.
- What you tried.
- What you expected.
- What happened instead.

Do not include private chat transcripts unless they are anonymized and needed to reproduce the problem.

## Bugs

Use the bug report template. Include reproduction steps and screenshots when they do not reveal private content.

## Security

Follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for vulnerability reports.
